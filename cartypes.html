Sedans

Sedans are among the most widely used vehicle models globally. They usually have a separate trunk for luggage in addition to four doors. The perfect blend of comfort, economy, and style characterizes sedans. The Honda and Toyota Camry are well-known models.

SUVs

SUVs, or sport utility vehicles, are multipurpose vehicles with roomy interiors and off-road capabilities. SUVs are preferred by both families and thrill-seekers due to their sturdy construction and cutting-edge safety features. The Ford Explorer, Jeep Grand Cherokee, and Toyota RAV4 are popular models.

Trucks

Trucks are renowned for their powerful construction and ability to tow. They are frequently utilized for both work and play. Some of the most popular automobiles sold worldwide are trucks, such as the Ford F-150, Chevrolet Silverado, and Ram 1500.

Electric Cars

For the auto industry, electric vehicles are the way of the future. They provide a more environmentally friendly and sustainable driving experience and are powered by electricity. The Tesla Model 3, Nissan Leaf, and Chevrolet Bolt EV are a few examples.
